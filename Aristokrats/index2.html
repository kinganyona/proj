About Us

Aristocrats Concrete Limited is a leading manufacturer and supplier of aggregates, civil engineering contractors, and a reliable partner in the construction industry. Operating a quarry at Katani, Syokimau, Kenya, we specialize in premix concrete, precast cement products, road construction, civil excavation, and urban blasting services. We are committed to quality, innovation, and delivering projects that meet our clients' expectations.

Our mission is to provide top-quality products and services that drive the development of infrastructure in Kenya and beyond. With a team of experienced professionals and state-of-the-art technology, we ensure the highest standards in every project we undertake.
